How We Collect & Classify Data

We collect only the information required to personalize living experiences, and we classify each element to apply the correct access controls.

Resident Relationship Data

Information you share when connecting with our leasing atelier or concierge.

  • Contact details collected through inquiries and tour bookings.
  • Lifestyle preferences gathered during consultations and welcome interviews.
  • Communication history maintained for service continuity.

Experience Signals

Insights that help us tailor programming, amenity usage, and concierge rituals.

  • Event RSVPs, amenity reservations, and concierge requests.
  • Voluntary surveys on design, art, culinary, or wellness interests.
  • Opt-in smart home telemetry used to automate in-residence comfort.

Digital Engagement

Analytics collected from our website, resident portal, and communications.

  • Session analytics with anonymized identifiers to enhance navigation.
  • Email interaction data to understand which updates are valuable.
  • Device and browser metadata for security monitoring.

All sensitive resident data is encrypted in transit and at rest, with access restricted to trained team members providing direct service to you.

How We Use Your Information

Your information powers personalized experiences while honoring your preferences.

Personalized Hospitality

  • Curating tours and design presentations aligned with your aesthetic.
  • Coordinating concierge services, amenity reservations, and resident events.
  • Delivering wellness, cultural, and culinary programming that matches your interests.

Communications You Control

  • Sending essential community updates and operational notices.
  • Sharing invitations to limited availability residences with your consent.
  • Providing opt-in lifestyle content curated by the Codex Collective.

You may update preferences or opt out of non-essential communications at any time from the resident portal or by contacting our privacy office.

Security Architecture

We apply multi-layer protections across people, process, and platforms.

Technical Controls

  • Zero-trust access with hardware keys for privileged systems.
  • Continuous vulnerability scanning and annual third-party penetration tests.
  • Separate encryption keys for resident financial and wellness data.

Operational Assurance

  • Background-checked staff complete quarterly privacy simulations.
  • Data handling runbooks aligned with SOC 2 Type II controls.
  • Incident response tabletop exercises every six months.

Resident Safeguards

  • Two-factor authentication and biometric access for portal and amenities.
  • Real-time alerts for new device logins or data export requests.
  • Dedicated privacy concierge to guide you through any request.

Data Lifecycle & Retention

Data is retained only as long as needed to provide services or fulfill legal obligations.

  1. 01

    Inquiry Submitted

    Contact details are stored securely for twelve months to coordinate tours unless you request deletion sooner.

    Retention: 12 months
  2. 02

    Residency Activation

    Onboarding documents and identification are encrypted and retained for the duration of your lease plus statutory periods.

    Retention: Lease term + 7 years
  3. 03

    Experience Personalization

    Preference insights and concierge notes are reviewed quarterly and removed when no longer relevant to your stay.

    Retention: Rolling 18 months
  4. 04

    Departure & Legacy Requests

    Within 30 days of move-out we archive non-essential data and anonymize engagement metrics.

    Retention: 30 days post move-out
